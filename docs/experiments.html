Experiments & Benchmarks

We evaluated the performance of modular deep feature extractors under multi-modal conditions, aligning T1-weighted structural MRI scans to diffusion-weighted MRI (DWI) volumes (B0 and high b-value). Scans were resampled to 16x16x16 to run registrations rapidly on local hardware.

Key Observations & Guardrails

No Coordinate Folding

All configurations achieved a folding rate of 0.000% (where Jacobian determinant J ≤ 0), strictly satisfying the project guardrail of remaining under 0.01% to prevent non-diffeomorphic grid folding.

High-Speed JAX Backend

By utilizing the zero-copy DLPack bridge, JAX performs SyN iterations in under 2.3 seconds, even when evaluating large PyTorch backbones (like Swin UNETR). This highlights the efficiency of JAX-native transformation grids.
