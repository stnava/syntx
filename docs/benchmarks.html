Deep Feature Extractors

- VGG19 (lncc_3d, Layer 4): Regularizes local grid folding down to 0.002% while maintaining accuracy levels comparable or superior to intensity LNCC.
- DINOv2 (ViT-S/14): Shows highly smooth warp fields, with zero folding rate (0.000%) across all test cases.
- ResNet-10 (MedicalNet): Evaluated directly in 3D feature space, converging in 45 seconds per volume.
- Swin UNETR: Leverages self-supervised transformer features to capture complex deformations.

Convergence & Settings

- Levels: 4 multi-resolution steps [8, 4, 2, 1].
